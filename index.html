About This Program

What Is Career Explorer?

Career Explorer uses a modified RIASEC (Holland Code) activity to help young learners connect current interests to real-world careers. It is an exploration tool—not a validated aptitude test, diagnosis, or prediction—and no result limits what a student can become.

How To Use This

Educator Instructions

Select the appropriate exploration level below and launch it on your classroom device or shared screen. Students answer based on what they genuinely enjoy, then review their strongest interest areas and any honest ties.
